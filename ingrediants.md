8. 1 tsp. of white venigar (5mL)

10. 2  to 2 1/2 Tbsp. of red food colouring liquid (30 to 40 mL)
11. 2 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour or cake flour (320g)
12. 1 tsp. of baking soda (5g)
13. 1 tsp. of baking salt (5g)
